Portfolio

Life Beyond Books - A Short Drama


Synopsis:


"Life Beyond Books" explores the contrasting approaches two students take towards exams. The narrative unfolds between two protagonists, one dedicated solely to studying, and the other balancing academics with extracurricular activities. The story delves into the consequences of their choices and challenges the conventional belief that academic success is solely determined by study hours.


Characters:


  • Sapuni (The Bookworm): A studious individual who dedicates most of their time to textbooks and exam preparation. Known for rigorous study routines and a focus solely on academics.
  • Nimnadi (The Balanced Achiever): A student who not only studies but also actively participates in extracurricular activities. Balancing academics with sports, arts, or other interests.

Key Scenes:


  • Study Sessions
  • Extracurricular Moments
  • Exam Day
  • Results Announcement

Themes:


  • Work-Life Balance
  • Holistic Development
  • Surprising Outcomes

Takeaway Message:


"Life Beyond Books" encourages viewers to appreciate the value of a balanced life, where personal growth and development are not confined to the pages of textbooks. Success can be achieved through a holistic approach that embraces both academic and extracurricular pursuits.
